Ruminations and remnants / the universe as a philosophical framework, part 1

Through science, we have uncovered a part of how our universe "works". They are known as The Scientific Laws of the Universe, and I've listed them below:

  1. Newton's Laws of Motion
  2. Law of Universal Gravitation
  3. Laws of Thermodynamics
  4. Einstein's Theory of Relativity
  5. Quantum Mechanics
  6. Conservation Laws

We limit the scope of these laws to "physics" or "sciences" and exclude them from our understanding of these laws as an understanding of the physics of the universe
